Environmental Attractiveness

perceived pleasantness and friendliness of an environment

Equal access to environmentally attractive greenspaces improves mental and physical wellbeing. We set out to assess North Leith Caledonian Railway Path’s (NLCRP) environmental attractiveness via fieldwork.
